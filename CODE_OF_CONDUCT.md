# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience level, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or technical preference.

## Expected behavior

Be respectful, precise, and constructive. Discuss ideas and evidence rather than people. Accept feedback, acknowledge mistakes, protect confidential information, and prioritize the safety of users and affected institutions.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, doxxing, publishing another person's private information, deliberate disruption, or using project channels to facilitate unlawful access to financial systems.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported privately to the repository maintainer through GitHub. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior is inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

This document is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1.
