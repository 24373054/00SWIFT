# 00SWIFT v3.0.0

Version 3 transforms 00SWIFT from a collection of SWIFT-style and e-CNY sandbox APIs into a standards-driven cross-border payment research platform.

## Highlights

- Versioned ISO 20022, CBPR+ SR2026, and CIPS research profiles with deterministic conformance vectors.
- Structured and hybrid postal address validation, Business Application Header integrity, and nine ISO 20022 message-family adapters.
- Durable payment lifecycles with UETR inheritance, transaction-copy hash chains, field lineage, investigations, SLAs, and data-quality analytics.
- Synthetic CIPS participant routing, direct-participant accounts, no-overdraft RTGS, atomic DNS net settlement, and cross-system Payment Lens events.
- Central-bank controlled two-tier e-CNY issuance and a synthetic Hong Kong retail profile.
- Offline-value vouchers, allow-listed programmable-money templates, multi-CBDC jurisdictions, bilateral policy enforcement, FX quotes, and atomic PvP settlement.
- Idempotency, transactional outbox, durable workflows, dead-letter states, database leases, PostgreSQL/Alembic, KMS/HSM abstraction, explainable policy decisions, trace contexts, hash-chained audit evidence, and reconciliation.

## Compatibility

- Python 3.11 and 3.12.
- SQLite remains supported for local sandbox development.
- PostgreSQL is validated by migration round-trip CI and is the recommended durable deployment database.
- Existing SWIFT-style and `/ecny/v1` sandbox routes remain available.
- New capabilities are exposed under `/nextgen/v1`.

## Security and scope

00SWIFT remains an independent research sandbox. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, connected to, or certified by SWIFT, CIPS, the People's Bank of China, HKMA, BIS Innovation Hub, or mBridge. The repository contains independently authored behavioral subsets and synthetic fixtures; it does not redistribute restricted implementation guides or production directories.

Do not use this release to move real funds or make automated legal, sanctions, AML, credit, or regulatory decisions.
